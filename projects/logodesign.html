Logo Design

This is a logo project that I did where I came up with three companies and I designed logos that were just designed with only images. I wanted to create something that is simple and easy to understand. When the viewer is looking at the image will they immediately understand what the logo they are looking at is for? One of the design principles that I used when I was working on this project was the negative space and focusing on using it to create contrast within the logos.
