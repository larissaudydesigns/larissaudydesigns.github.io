Religious Documents

For this project I created a passion project with a document that is important to me. My goal was to use the principles of design to create something that was simple and had a kind of modern feel to it. Something that you can hang up in your home. I decided to have the phrases that were important in bold so that they stand out when the viewer is reading them and also so if they are scanning it looking for a specific part they will be able to easily find it.
