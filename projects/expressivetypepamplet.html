Expressive Type Pamphlet

For this project I determined the topic that I was interested in and then when I was laying out the design of the book the main goal was to use the typography to communicate the message and mood of the book. Since the theme of the book was about money I decided to use a font that was similar to the one on the dollar bill. I also used hierarchy and alignment to make sure that the viewer understands the importance and message of the book. I also wanted it to be something that was simple but looks complex.
